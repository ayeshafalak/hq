Sanjivini Seva Jankalyan Samiti

Sanjivni Seva Jankalyan Samiti was founded in March 2014. which is now a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO).

About Us


In the society of wealthy and elite, the lives of some unfortunate has lag behind, Sanjeevni Seva has come forward with a selfless heart to heal the hardship by offering a helping hand to needy ones. The foundation was laid in 2014 with its very first event of Blood Donation and gradually the organization caught the pace by accomplishing various responsibilities towards society. From donating blood to providing education to specially abled children, from distributing blankets to celebrating festivals with underprivilege societies, Sanjeevni Seva has come a long way and will keep touching the hearts.
We started this by donating blood to the patients, and with time we started teaching, paying fee for the poor children, provided them stationary, groceries etc.

Vision

Mission


HELP is the core mission of our organisation. We believe every segment of our society is a pearl for the nation & by giving them fair chance we are contributing in the upliftment of the society. Every day we rise with an aim to help the needy and eradicate the paucity of our nation.
